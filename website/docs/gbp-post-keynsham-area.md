# GBP post: Keynsham, Bath – painter & decorator

Use this when posting about your **Keynsham** coverage on **Google Business Profile** (Google Business → Posts → Create post). Targets "painter decorator Keynsham" and "decorator Keynsham" to boost the map pack in that area. For a post that also lists Saltford, Bitton, Whitchurch and nearby villages, use **`gbp-post-keynsham-area-sub-areas.md`** instead.

## 1. Image (optional but recommended)

- **Option A:** Use a photo of a job in Keynsham (BS31) — period cottage, exterior, finished room. Resize to **1080 × 1080 px** (square) or **1080 × 1350 px** (portrait). Minimum width **720 px**.
- **Option B:** Your logo or a simple graphic with "Keynsham, Bath" and "Painter & decorator".
- **Option C:** A local job photo from Keynsham or BS31. Avoid stock images that don't reflect your work.

## 5. In Google Business Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and open your **New Decorating** profile.
2. Click **Posts** (or **Updates** / **Add update**).
3. **Create post** → choose **Update**.
4. **Add photo** → upload your image (job in Keynsham, logo, or area graphic).
5. **Headline** → paste one of the headlines above.
6. **Description** → paste the description (or shorter version).
7. **Button** → set label (e.g. "Get a quote" or "View area") and URL: `https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/keynsham` or `https://new-decorating.co.uk/contact`.
8. **Publish**.
