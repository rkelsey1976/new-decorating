# GBP post: Odd Down, Bath – painter & decorator

Use this when posting about your **Odd Down** coverage on **Google Business Profile** (Google Business → Posts → Create post). Targets "painter decorator Odd Down" and "decorator Odd Down Bath" to boost the map pack in that area.

---

## 1. Image (optional but recommended)

- **Option A:** Use a photo of a job in Odd Down or south Bath (e.g. semi, exterior, finished room). Resize to **1080 × 1080 px** (square) or **1080 × 1350 px** (portrait). Minimum width **720 px**.
- **Option B:** Your logo or a simple graphic with "Odd Down, Bath" and "Painter & decorator".
- **Option C:** A local job photo from Odd Down or south Bath. Avoid stock images that don't reflect your work.

---

## 2. Post type

- **Update** (standard post) – best for "areas we cover" and local visibility.

---

## 3. Copy for the post

**Headline** (keep under ~58 characters):

```
Painter & decorator in Odd Down, Bath
```

**Alternative headline:**

```
Odd Down, Bath – painter and decorator, 25+ years
```

**Description** (paste into the post text):

```
I'm a painter and decorator covering Odd Down and south Bath. Odd Down has a mix of 1930s semis, post-war housing and newer developments — I've been working here for over 25 years.

Interior and exterior painting, wallpaper, preparation and repair. Full house repaints, single rooms, exterior work on pebbledash and render. Every job is quoted individually with a free, no-obligation quote. Get in touch to confirm I cover your street.
```

**Shorter option** (if character limit is tight):

```
Painter and decorator in Odd Down, Bath. Interior, exterior, wallpaper. 25+ years. Free quotes.
```

## 4. Call-to-action button

- **Button text:** `Get a quote` or `Free quote` or `View area`
- **Link (Odd Down area page):** `https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/odd-down`
- **Link (contact):** `https://new-decorating.co.uk/contact`

Use the **area page** to send people to your Odd Down page (good for local SEO); use **contact** for a direct quote CTA.

(Add in GBP when it asks for "Button" or "Call to action".)

---

## 5. In Google Business Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and open your **New Decorating** profile.
2. Click **Posts** (or **Updates** / **Add update**).
3. **Create post** → choose **Update**.
4. **Add photo** → upload your image (job in Odd Down, logo, or area graphic).
5. **Headline** → paste one of the headlines above.
6. **Description** → paste the description (or shorter version).
7. **Button** → set label (e.g. "Get a quote" or "View area") and URL: `https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/odd-down` or `https://new-decorating.co.uk/contact`.
8. **Publish**.

---

## 6. Why this helps the map pack

- **Local SEO:** Reinforces "Odd Down" and "Bath" on your GBP so Google associates you with Odd Down for "painter decorator Odd Down" and "decorator Odd Down" searches.
- **Clarity:** Shows you actively work in Odd Down, not just "Bath" in general.
- **Consistency:** Matches your website's Odd Down area page ([/area/odd-down](https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/odd-down)), so GBP and site send the same local signals.
