# GBP post: Weston, Bath – painter & decorator

Use this when posting about your **Weston** coverage on **Google Business Profile** (Google Business → Posts → Create post). Targets "painter decorator Weston" and "decorator Weston Bath" to boost the map pack in that area.

---

## 1. Image (optional but recommended)

- **Option A:** Use a photo of a job in Weston or north-west Bath (e.g. terrace, exterior, finished room). Resize to **1080 × 1080 px** (square) or **1080 × 1350 px** (portrait). Minimum width **720 px**.
- **Option B:** Your logo or a simple graphic with "Weston, Bath" and "Painter & decorator".
- **Option C:** A local job photo from Weston. Avoid stock images that don't reflect your work.

---

## 2. Post type

- **Update** (standard post) – best for "areas we cover" and local visibility.

---

## 3. Copy for the post

**Headline** (keep under ~58 characters):

```
Painter & decorator in Weston, Bath
```

**Alternative headline:**

```
Weston, Bath – painter and decorator, 25+ years
```

**Description** (paste into the post text):

```
I'm a painter and decorator covering Weston and north-west Bath. Weston has a mix of Victorian and Edwardian terraces, family homes and modern housing. I've been painting and decorating here for over 25 years.

Interior and exterior painting, wallpaper, preparation and repair. Full house and single room projects — I'm local, tidy and always provide a free quote before starting. Get in touch to confirm I cover your street.
```

**Shorter option** (if character limit is tight):

```
Painter and decorator in Weston, Bath. Interior, exterior, wallpaper. 25+ years. Free quotes.
```

---

## 4. Call-to-action button

- **Button text:** `Get a quote` or `Free quote` or `View area`
- **Link (Weston area page):** `https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/weston`
- **Link (contact):** `https://new-decorating.co.uk/contact`

Use the **area page** to send people to your Weston page (good for local SEO); use **contact** for a direct quote CTA.

(Add in GBP when it asks for "Button" or "Call to action".)

---

## 5. In Google Business Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and open your **New Decorating** profile.
2. Click **Posts** (or **Updates** / **Add update**).
3. **Create post** → choose **Update**.
4. **Add photo** → upload your image (job in Weston, logo, or area graphic).
5. **Headline** → paste one of the headlines above.
6. **Description** → paste the description (or shorter version).
7. **Button** → set label (e.g. "Get a quote" or "View area") and URL: `https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/weston` or `https://new-decorating.co.uk/contact`.
8. **Publish**.

---

## 6. Why this helps the map pack

- **Local SEO:** Reinforces "Weston" and "Bath" on your GBP so Google associates you with Weston for "painter decorator Weston" and "decorator Weston Bath" searches.
- **Clarity:** Shows you actively work in Weston, not just "Bath" in general.
- **Consistency:** Matches your website's Weston area page ([/area/weston](https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/weston)), so GBP and site send the same local signals.
