## Tips for your Yelp listing

1. **Claim the listing** – If Yelp already has a page for “New Decorating” in Bath, claim it so you can edit details and respond to reviews.  
2. **Category** – Choose “Painters” or “Painting & Wall Covering” as the primary category so you show up in the right searches.  
3. **Ask happy customers for reviews** – After a job, send them your Yelp page link and ask for a review (same as you do for Google).  
4. **Respond to reviews** – Thank people for positive reviews and reply professionally to any criticism.  
5. **Keep details up to date** – If your phone, area or services change, update your Yelp listing and your website together.
