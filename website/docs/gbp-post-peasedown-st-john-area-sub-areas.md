# GBP post: Peasedown St John area – sub-areas we work in

Use this when posting about your **Peasedown St John** and **Somer Valley** coverage on **Google Business Profile** (Google Business → Posts → Create post). Highlights Peasedown St John and the surrounding villages you cover.

---

## 1. Image (optional but recommended)

- **Option A:** Use a photo of a job in Peasedown St John or the Somer Valley (e.g. stone cottage, exterior, finished room). Resize to **1080 × 1080 px** (square) or **1080 × 1350 px** (portrait). Minimum width **720 px**.
- **Option B:** Your logo or a simple graphic with “Peasedown St John & Somer Valley” and a short list (e.g. Midsomer Norton, Radstock, Paulton).
- **Option C:** A local job photo from the Somer Valley. Avoid stock images that don’t reflect your work.

---

## 2. Post type

- **Update** (standard post) – best for “areas we cover” and local visibility.

---

## 3. Copy for the post

**Headline** (keep under ~58 characters):

```
Painter & decorator in Peasedown St John & Somer Valley
```

**Alternative headline:**

```
Peasedown St John, Midsomer Norton, Radstock – painter
```

**Description** (paste into the post text):

```
I'm a painter and decorator covering Peasedown St John and the surrounding Somer Valley. I work in Peasedown St John, Midsomer Norton, Radstock, Paulton, Chilcompton, Westfield, Stratton-on-the-Fosse, Writhlington and the surrounding villages.

Interior and exterior painting, wallpaper, preparation and repair. Stone cottages, Victorian terraces and modern homes — I've been working in the area for over 25 years. Every job is quoted individually with a free, no-obligation quote. Get in touch to confirm I cover your street.
```

**Shorter option** (if character limit is tight):

```
Painter and decorator in Peasedown St John, Midsomer Norton, Radstock, Paulton, Chilcompton, Westfield, Stratton. Somer Valley. Interior, exterior, wallpaper. Free quotes.
```

- **Button text:** `View areas` or `Get a quote` or `Free quote`

Use the **area page** if you want to send people to the full Peasedown St John + surrounding areas list; use **contact** for a direct quote CTA.

(Add in GBP when it asks for “Button” or “Call to action”.)

---

## 5. In Google Business Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and open your **New Decorating** profile.
2. Click **Posts** (or **Updates** / **Add update**).
3. **Create post** → choose **Update**.
4. **Add photo** → upload your image (job in Somer Valley, logo, or area graphic).
5. **Headline** → paste one of the headlines above.
6. **Description** → paste the description (or shorter version).
7. **Button** → set label (e.g. “View areas” or “Get a quote”) and URL: `https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/peasedown-st-john` or `https://new-decorating.co.uk/contact`.
8. **Publish**.

---

## 6. Why this helps

- **Local SEO:** Reinforces “Peasedown St John”, “Somer Valley”, “Midsomer Norton”, “Radstock” and other village names on your GBP.
- **Clarity:** Shows you cover the whole Somer Valley, not just one village.
- **Consistency:** Matches your website’s Peasedown St John area page ([/area/peasedown-st-john](https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/peasedown-st-john)), which lists the same surrounding areas with links.
