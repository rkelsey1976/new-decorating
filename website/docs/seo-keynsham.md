## Optional improvements

1. **Google Business Profile**  
   If you have a GBP, add Keynsham (and BS31) in service areas and in the description. Consistent NAP and reviews help local pack for "painter Keynsham".

2. **Directory listings**  
   Use "Keynsham" and "BS31" in business descriptions on FreeIndex, Yelp, Bing Places, etc. (See `business-directories-checklist.md` and **Local Keynsham directories** below.)

3. **More Keynsham content over time**  
   Add a sentence or short paragraph to a future blog post (e.g. "In Keynsham and Saltford I often…") to reinforce relevance without stuffing.

4. **Image alt text**  
   Where it fits, use "Interior painting Keynsham", "Exterior painting Keynsham" or "Decorating Keynsham" in gallery/service image alt text.

5. **Backlinks**  
   Local backlinks (Keynsham community sites, local business listings, local news) can help. Focus on quality and relevance over quantity.
