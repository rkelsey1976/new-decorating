## Bark-specific tips

- **Lead model:** You receive leads from customers; you can review them before paying. Only pay for leads you want to pursue.
- **Profile completeness:** Fill in every section (description, services, areas, photos) so your profile ranks better and customers trust you.
- **Photos:** Add photos of finished work (interior/exterior, feature walls, woodwork) to improve response rates.
- **Response time:** Replying quickly to leads can improve your position and win more jobs.
- **NAP:** Use the same business name, phone and website as on your site and GBP so your listing supports local SEO.
