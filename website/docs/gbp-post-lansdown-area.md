# GBP post: Lansdown, Bath – painter & decorator

Use this when posting about your **Lansdown** coverage on **Google Business Profile** (Google Business → Posts → Create post). Targets "painter decorator Lansdown" and "decorator Lansdown Bath" to boost the map pack in that area.

---

## 1. Image (optional but recommended)

- **Option A:** Use a photo of a job on Lansdown or north Bath (e.g. period property, exterior, finished room). Resize to **1080 × 1080 px** (square) or **1080 × 1350 px** (portrait). Minimum width **720 px**.
- **Option B:** Your logo or a simple graphic with "Lansdown, Bath" and "Painter & decorator".
- **Option C:** A local job photo from the Lansdown area. Avoid stock images that don't reflect your work.

---

## 2. Post type

- **Update** (standard post) – best for "areas we cover" and local visibility.

---

## 3. Copy for the post

**Headline** (keep under ~58 characters):

```
Painter & decorator in Lansdown, Bath
```

**Alternative headline:**

```
Lansdown, Bath – painter and decorator, 25+ years
```

**Description** (paste into the post text):

```
I'm a painter and decorator covering Lansdown and north Bath. Lansdown has a mix of period homes, larger properties and character housing — I've been working in the area for over 25 years.

Interior and exterior painting, wallpaper, preparation and repair. Bath stone, period features and modern finishes. Every job is quoted individually with a free, no-obligation quote. Get in touch to confirm I cover your street.
```

**Shorter option** (if character limit is tight):

```
Painter and decorator in Lansdown, Bath. Interior, exterior, wallpaper. 25+ years. Free quotes.
```

---

## 4. Call-to-action button

- **Button text:** `Get a quote` or `Free quote` or `View area`
- **Link (Lansdown area page):** `https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/lansdown`
- **Link (contact):** `https://new-decorating.co.uk/contact`

Use the **area page** to send people to your Lansdown page (good for local SEO); use **contact** for a direct quote CTA.

(Add in GBP when it asks for "Button" or "Call to action".)

---

## 5. In Google Business Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and open your **New Decorating** profile.
2. Click **Posts** (or **Updates** / **Add update**).
3. **Create post** → choose **Update**.
4. **Add photo** → upload your image (job on Lansdown, logo, or area graphic).
5. **Headline** → paste one of the headlines above.
6. **Description** → paste the description (or shorter version).
7. **Button** → set label (e.g. "Get a quote" or "View area") and URL: `https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/lansdown` or `https://new-decorating.co.uk/contact`.
8. **Publish**.

---

## 6. Why this helps the map pack

- **Local SEO:** Reinforces "Lansdown" and "Bath" on your GBP so Google associates you with Lansdown for "painter decorator Lansdown" and "decorator Lansdown Bath" searches.
- **Clarity:** Shows you actively work in Lansdown, not just "Bath" in general.
- **Consistency:** Matches your website's Lansdown area page ([/area/lansdown](https://new-decorating.co.uk/area/lansdown)), so GBP and site send the same local signals.
